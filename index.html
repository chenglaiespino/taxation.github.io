Evolution of Taxation: Insights from Philippine History

It conveys the exploration of how taxation systems in the Philippines have transformed over time. It delves into the historical progression and changes in tax policies, practices, and their significance across different periods in Philippine history, offering valuable perspectives and understandings regarding the development and impact of taxation within the country.
